A database of dramaturgical choices made in film adaptations of Shakespeare plays.

In other words: a one-stop-shop to compare directorial, casting, acting, and editing decisions made in Shakespeare films.

Academics, film buffs, theatre fans, and anyone who likes clicking through neat data.

Please contact me if you'd like to contribute to the site!

Film metadata is from MovieDB, Shakespeare play data is from Folger Shakespeare, and Questions and Interpretations are tracked and uploaded by volunteers. (Currently, just me.)

See the acknowledgments page for more details.
